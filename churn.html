Customer Churn Prediction Model

Type: Supervised Machine Learning

Tools: Python, Pandas, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib

Overview

This project involved building a predictive model to classify customers at risk of churning from a subscription-based service. It focused on identifying key indicators of churn to support business retention strategies.

What I Did

I cleaned and prepared the dataset, explored features, and built a logistic regression model using Scikit-learn. I also evaluated model performance using metrics like accuracy, confusion matrix, and ROC-AUC score. Visuals were created to show the distribution of churned customers across key variables.

Impact

The project demonstrated my ability to apply predictive analytics to a real-world business scenario. It also helped me understand how data science can guide retention efforts and customer experience strategies.
